Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter

Method

  1. Melt butter ina frying pan overa medium heat
  2. Gently Mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. once the butter is melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula food the eggs from the edge of the pan to teh center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
